About The Event

The Most Prestigious Creative Awards

The One Show is a global awards show honoring the year's best in advertising, design, and digital marketing. Since its inception, it has recognized creative excellence across all media.

In 2025, we're bringing together the most innovative minds in the industry for three days of inspiration, networking, and celebration.

This year's theme, "Creative Without Borders," focuses on how great ideas transcend geographical, cultural, and technological boundaries to make a lasting impact.

Award Categories

Traditional Advertising

Recognizing excellence in print, outdoor, radio, and television advertising that demonstrates outstanding creativity and effectiveness.

Digital & Interactive

Celebrating innovative digital campaigns, websites, apps, and interactive experiences that engage audiences in meaningful ways.

Design & Branding

Honoring exceptional design work including logos, packaging, environmental design, and comprehensive branding systems.
